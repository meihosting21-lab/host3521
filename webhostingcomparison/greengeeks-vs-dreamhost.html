Core Philosophy

GreenGeeks proves that being "Eco-Friendly" doesn't mean being slow. They match 300% of their energy consumption with renewable energy credits. But under the hood, they are a beast: they use the same LiteSpeed architecture as high-end performance hosts. They are perfect for users who want top-tier speed while reducing their carbon footprint.

DreamHost operates on a philosophy of "Total Freedom." While most hosts hide caps in the fine print, DreamHost is one of the few that truly offers Unlimited Traffic and Storage. Furthermore, they are so confident in their infrastructure that they offer a 100% Uptime Guarantee (industry standard is 99.9%). If your site goes down, they pay you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GreenGeeks actually fast or just eco-friendly?
It is both. GreenGeeks doesn't sacrifice speed for the environment. They use the same high-performance LiteSpeed servers as other top-tier hosts. You get blazing fast load times and the peace of mind that your website is carbon-negative.
I don't want to pay for a full year. Which one is better?
DreamHost is the winner here. Unlike most hosts that force you into 12 or 36-month contracts to get a decent price, DreamHost offers very affordable month-to-month plans. It's perfect if you want to test the waters without a long-term commitment.
Is "Unlimited" traffic really unlimited?
Most hosts have hidden caps, but DreamHost is famous for being truly uncapped on bandwidth and storage for shared hosting. As long as your site is personal or small business (not a file-sharing farm), they won't throttle you.
